# Socoeur — Style Reference

## Photographer Reference
Gentian Kadrijaj (@gentiankadrijaj)

---

## Visual Style Analysis

### Light
- Outdoor: direct natural light, unfiltered, raw, high contrast
- Studio: directional artificial light, deep defined shadows
- No diffusion, no softening

### Color & Palette
- Natural, slightly desaturated outdoors
- Studio: intentional solid color backgrounds (not applicable for Socoeur)
- Subject is always the strongest color element in frame
- Visible film grain on several shots

### Composition
- Dramatic angles — low angle, high angle, contre-plongée
- Subject fills the frame with intention
- Simple or architectural backgrounds that reinforce the subject
- Geometric repetition when architecture is the subject

### Energy
- Movement captured mid-action
- Confident posture, never static
- Urban and cultural attitude, never commercial

---

## What Applies to Socoeur

### Use
- Outdoor natural lighrban/concrete backgrounds
- Dramatic low or high angles
- Architecture as pure subject (geometric repetition)
- Subtle film grain
- Raw, unfiltered confidence

### Avoid for Sprint 1
- Saturated studio color backgrounds
- Extreme movement or hair-in-motion
- Tight face-only compositions

## CapCut Consistency Rules

- Same color preset on every clip (warm neutral, slight desaturation)
- No aggressive transitions
- Clean cuts only
- Duration: 6-8 seconds per clip
- On-screen text: Satoshi or neutral sans-serif, f-white, max 3-5 words
