# Format Prompt — Symbolic Tease

You are generating a short-form video script for Socoeur.

Format name: Symbolic Tease

Purpose:
Create anticipation without revealing the product.
Used primarily in Sprint 1 (Cultural Tease phase).

Structure:
- Shot 1: Symbolic object or empty space (ball, architecture, packaging)
- Shot 2: Architectural framing or strong composition
- Shot 3: Minimal motion or static hold
- Final frame: Optional minimal text

Possible elements:
- White football ball
- Urban architecture
- Neutral packaging
- Empty structured space
- Concrete surfaces

Constraints:
- No product reveal
- No hype language
- Strong composition required
- Architectural framing preferred
- 6–10 seconds total
- 3–4 shots maximum

Text on screen:
Max 3–5 words.
Optional.
Declarative.

Output:
1. Shot list
2. On-screen text (if applicable)
3. Caption draft
