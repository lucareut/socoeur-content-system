# Trend Research Prompt — Cultural & Viral Analysis

You are analyzing current Instagram and TikTok trends
relevant to a contemporary cultural fashion brand based in Switzerland.

Target context:
- Urban Europe
- Cultural streetwear
- Fashion-led brands referencing football
- Minimal aesthetic
- Architecture-driven visuals
- Neutral tones with strong accent color

Audience:
25–40 years old
Urban
Culturally aware
Interested in fashion, football culture, design

Your task:

1. Identify emerging short-form video patterns
   - Editing rhythm
   - Camera movement
   - Framing style
   - Use of text
   - Sound usage

2. Identify viral but adaptable concepts that:
   - Do NOT rely on hype behavior
   - Do NOT rely on loud personality
   - Can be executed with minimal production
   - Maintain a refined aesthetic

3. Highlight recurring visual codes in:
   - Aimé Leon Dore
   - Arte Antwerp
   - Adidas Originals
   - Contemporary European streetwear brands

4. Suggest 5 adaptable video concepts for Socoeur,
   respecting:
   - Brand Core constraints
   - Minimal tone
   - Cultural positioning
   - No hype behavior

For each concept, provide:
- Trend insight
- Why it works
- How to adapt it for Socoeur
- Risk to avoid

Avoid:
- TikTok gimmicks
- Meme culture
- Overly commercial hooks
- Influencer-centric formats

Output must be structured and strategic.
